How the Product Works

  • ✓ Vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol) is a vital fat-soluble vitamin that plays a key role in regulating calcium-phosphorus metabolism, ensuring the strength of bones and teeth.
  • ✓ Strengthens the immune system, promotes the body's resistance to infections.
  • ✓ Participates in maintaining normal functioning of the cardiovascular system and nerve conduction.
  • ✓ Helps reduce the risk of inflammation and autoimmune diseases.

Main Indications for Use

  • ✓ Vitamin D deficiency (confirmed by analysis or presence of risk factors).
  • ✓ Support for bone health (prevention of osteoporosis).
  • ✓ Weakened immune system, frequent colds.
  • ✓ Periods of insufficient sun exposure (winter, indoor work).

Usage Recommendations

  • ✓ Adults: 1 capsule per day during meals (preferably with fatty foods for better absorption).

Contraindications

  • ✖ Individual intolerance to components.

  • ✖ Severe forms of kidney disease.
